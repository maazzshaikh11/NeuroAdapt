# NeuroAdapt Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

## Privacy Policy

NeuroAdapt ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the NeuroAdapt Chrome Extension and its associated web dashboard.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

* Full name
* Email address
* Encrypted authentication credentials

### Accessibility Preferences

We store accessibility preferences including:

* Reading simplification level
* Theme preferences
* Font preferences
* Bionic Reading settings
* Focus Mode settings

These preferences are synchronized across your devices when you are signed in.

### AI Text Processing

When you choose to simplify text:

* Only the text you explicitly select is sent securely to our backend for processing.
* The selected text is processed solely to generate a simplified version.
* We do not continuously monitor or collect webpage content.

### Usage Analytics

To improve NeuroAdapt, we may collect limited anonymous usage statistics such as:

* Number of simplifications performed
* Feature usage
* Reading preferences
* Performance metrics

We do not collect your browsing history.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Provide accessibility features
* Synchronize preferences across devices
* Improve the quality of AI-generated simplifications
* Enhance application performance
* Maintain account security

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information is only processed by trusted service providers necessary to operate NeuroAdapt, including secure cloud hosting and AI processing services.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

## Your Rights

You may:

* Update your profile information
* Modify accessibility preferences
* Delete your account
* Contact us regarding your personal data

## Children's Privacy

NeuroAdapt is not intended for children under 13 without parental supervision.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the NeuroAdapt website.
